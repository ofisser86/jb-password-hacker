Even numbers

Define a generator even that produces even numbers (0, 2, 4, 6, ...).

For a given number n, print out the first n ones on separate lines.


Sample Input:
2

Sample Output:
0
2


Sample Input:
5

Sample Output:
0
2
4
6
8



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 15 seconds
