Squares

Define a generator squares that produces an infinite sequence of the squares of all natural numbers (1, 4, 9, 16, ...).

For a given number n, print out the first n elements each on a new line.


Sample Input:
2

Sample Output:
1
4


Sample Input:
5

Sample Output:
1
4
9
16
25



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 15 seconds
